Napkin technology company


The Napkin technology company is an open-source based startup developing visualization software in the tech-industry. The company is based in Norway, with offices in Stavanger, but aims for a global market-share in the visualization sector.

The company

Napkin AS was founded in 2019 by a group of technology enthusiasts wanting to make the experience of visualizing geographic data more user-friendly and accessible. Based on these founding principles the Napkin team started developing solutions, based entirely on open-source software, with the aim of taking data-visualization to the next level. With the release of Napkin-Visual in 2020, the Napkin company aim to take a market share of the data-visualization sector world-wide.
