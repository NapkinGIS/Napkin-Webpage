Napkin technology company


The Napkin technology company is an open-source based startup developing visualization software, along with GIS and blockchain services in the tech-industry. The company is based in Norway, with offices in Stavanger, but aims for a global market-share in the GIS and visualization sectors.

The company

Napkin AS was founded in 2019 by a group of technology enthusiasts wanting to make the experience of working with GIS and blockchain software more user-friendly and accessible. Based on these founding principles the Napkin team started developing solutions, based entirely on open-source software, with the aim of taking data-visualization to the next level. With the release of Napkin-Visual in 2020, the Napkin company aim to take a market share of the data-visualization sector world-wide.

Team

The Napkin team has many years combined experience working with GIS solutions in industry. We have built up unique experience working with open-source GIS software, and these delivering FOSS solutions in critical business areas. We are engineers with a diverse range of expertise who love to create software that works for the user, instead of against. Our founder and CEO, Andreas Atakan, has based the company on the principle of software designed for humans.
