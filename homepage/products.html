High powered visualizations


Napkin-Visual is a powerful and intuitive spatial analysis tool that enables you to gain priceless insight into your data. It is a web-based map solution that can be deployed and integrated anywhere, from websites to mobile apps.

Insight into historical data

Napkin-Visual can visualize historical data using a dynamic timeline, giving the user an easy and intuitive understanding of critical data-events. The following animation shows how to easily navigate large data-sets in Napkin-Visual.

Dynamic and real-time data

In addition to historical data, Napkin-Visual can also handle real-time and dynamic data. This gives Napkin-Visual an unrivaled power as a presentation and insight tool.

GIS services


The Napkin team has many years combined experience working with GIS solutions in industry. We have built up unique experience working with open-source GIS software, and these delivering FOSS solutions in critical business areas. Based on our many years experience and unique open-source focus, we offer GIS services with an emphasis on reliability and longevity.

Open-source GIS

Our many years experience has given us expertise in the full open-source GIS stack the OSS community offers. We have particular expertise with the following components:


Database
PostGIS, leading open-source geospacial database
Data serving
GeoServer, fully OGC compliant server
Analysis
QGIS desktop, most popular open-source GIS software
Back-end
QGIS server, seamlessly integrated back-end
Field app
QField, a fully featured field app
